First Quarter Reflection

For my first quarter I had a lot of fun learning how to do different things using HTML.
I learned how to make tables, lists, forms and how to edit them to my tastes.
For the practical exams I struggled a bit since I was new to the attributes and the tags.
But for written exams I did better, surely not the best in my definition but I felt a difference between the two.

For the second quarter I will no longer procrastinate my activities
and cram them the day of the submission date like what I am doing right now (I love you Ms. Uminga).
I will study harder and I will persevere in my academics while maintaining an interest in ICT as a subject and the topics being discussed in it.
I had a lot of fun working with my friends and fellow classmates in during class and
I hope to encounter fun activities such as the Daily Tasks and the pairworks and more in the future in our ICT classes.
